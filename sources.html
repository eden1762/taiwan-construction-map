現況收錄規則Active collection rules

首頁只放現在仍值得追的工程。The homepage keeps projects that are current and useful.

納入計畫中、評估中、設計中、施工中，以及完工或啟用一個月內的工程。取消、停辦、來源失效、無法查證、重複或明顯過期的資料，不混進現況統計。The active view includes planned, under-evaluation, in-design, under-construction, and recently completed or opened projects. Canceled, suspended, inactive, unverified, duplicate, or clearly outdated records stay out of the current overview.
