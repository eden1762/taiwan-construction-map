現況收錄規則Active collection rules

首頁只放現在仍有參考價值的工程。The homepage keeps projects that are still current and useful.

可納入首頁的狀態包含計畫中、評估中、設計中、施工中，以及完工或啟用一個月內。已取消、停辦、來源失效、無法查證、重複或明顯過期的資料不進首頁現況統計。Homepage records include planned, under-evaluation, in-design, under-construction, and recently completed or opened projects. Canceled, suspended, inactive, unverified, duplicate, or clearly outdated records are kept out of the active homepage view.

五大工程圖層Five map layers

  • 公共工程、道路/管線、捷運/交通、建築/園區、規劃/環評。
  • 點位顏色依圖層區分，搜尋可查工程名、地點、甲方與乙方。
  • Public works, roads/utilities, metro/transport, buildings/parks, and planning/environmental review.
  • Pin colors follow layers; search supports project name, place, owner, and contractor.

九大資料面向Nine data aspects

  • 基本資料、位置範圍、工程內容、發包與廠商、金額成本、時間期程、施工進度、審查法規、民眾資訊。
  • 查不到的欄位標示「待補資料 / To be verified」，不臆測。
  • Basics, location range, scope, owner and contractor, budget, schedule, progress, review rules, and public information.
  • Unknown fields are marked “To be verified” rather than guessed.

官方入口Official portals

查核時優先回到可公開追溯的來源。Verification starts from traceable public portals.

下列入口提供工程公告、採購、施工進度、交通建設、水利防災、港灣機場、社宅園區、環境審查、建照使照、道路挖掘與地理圖資線索；每筆工程正式收錄前仍需逐案比對名稱、位置、狀態、期程、甲方、乙方與來源日期。These portals provide leads for project notices, procurement, progress, transport works, water and resilience works, port and airport works, housing and park projects, environmental review, building and occupancy permits, road excavation, and geospatial data. Each record still needs project-by-project checks before it enters the active map.
