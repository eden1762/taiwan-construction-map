地圖操作提示Map tips
  • 手機可直接拖曳地圖、點縣市標記，或搜尋工程名、地點、甲方與乙方。
  • 桌機可用右下角 + / − 調整視角，再點工程點位查看金額、期程與來源。
  • 若底圖暫時較慢，工程清單、分類入口、縣市卡片與台灣簡圖會先保持可用。
  • On mobile, drag the map, tap city markers, or search by project, place, owner, or contractor.
  • On desktop, use + / − to adjust the view, then tap a project pin to check budget, schedule, and source.
  • If the basemap is slow, the project list, sector entry points, city cards, and Taiwan fallback map stay ready first.

現況資料守則Active data rules

首頁現況統計只納入計畫中、評估中、設計中、施工中，或完工／啟用一個月內的工程；過期、取消、停辦或來源失效的資料會移出首頁，避免把歷史案誤看成現況工程。

Active overview keeps planned, under-evaluation, in-design, under-construction, or completed/opened-within-one-month projects only. Outdated or inactive records are moved out of the homepage view.

資料來源怎麼看How to read sources
  • 來源連結優先放可回查的主管機關、發包單位、公告或公開圖資入口。
  • 工程金額、期程、甲方與乙方可能隨招標、變更設計或公告更新；正式引用前請回來源確認最新版。
  • 資料會逐步輪流補進不同主管機關、不同縣市與不同工程類型，避免只看單一來源。
  • 沒有公開佐證的欄位會標示「待補資料 / To be verified」，不把猜測內容混進工程卡片。
  • Source links point first to traceable owner agencies, project owners, public notices, or public map portals.
  • Budgets, schedules, owners, and contractors may change with tendering or design updates; re-check the source before formal use.
  • New records are rotated across agencies, cities, and project sectors so the map does not lean on only one source.
  • Fields without public evidence are marked “To be verified,” and guesses are kept out of project cards.
